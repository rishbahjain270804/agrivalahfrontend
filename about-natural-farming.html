India's Solution ‚Äî The National Natural Farming Mission (NNFM)

The Government of India has launched the National Natural Farming Mission to help farmers return to nature-based cultivation and restore soil and human health.

Through the PGS-India Natural & Organic Farming Certification System, every farmer can now:

  • Grow chemical-free crops that heal soil and improve health
  • Obtain an official Natural Farming Certificate under Govt. of India norms
  • Earn better market prices with verified natural produce
  • Get access to training on Jeevamrut, Beejamrut, composting, and bio-inputs
  • Build a digital farmer identity on pgsindia-ncof.gov.in, trusted by buyers across India

Why Certification Matters

For years, farmers have been told to "go organic." But without proof, without verification, "organic" often became just another word on a label.

Now, under the National Natural Farming Mission (#NNFM) and PGS-India, certification gives that word meaning.

Your label now guarantees that the food you eat ‚Äî and the soil it came from ‚Äî are both chemical-free, healthy, and traceable.

Certification isn't a formality. It's a farmer's identity ‚Äî proof of commitment to soil, community, and country.
