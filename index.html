Frequently Asked Questions

What is PGS-India Natural Farming Certification?
PGS-India (Participatory Guarantee System) is a government-backed certification program that verifies natural farming practices. It ensures your produce meets organic standards without the use of harmful chemicals, making it market-ready and premium-priced.
How much does the certification cost?
Our certification package costs only ₹300, which includes farm inspection, documentation, certification issuance, and one-year validity. This is significantly lower than traditional organic certification costs.
How long does the certification process take?
The entire certification process typically takes 15-20 days from application submission. Our team conducts a thorough farm inspection and documentation review before issuing the certificate.
What are the benefits of natural farming certification?
Certified natural farming products command 30-50% higher market prices, gain access to premium markets, qualify for government subsidies, improve soil health, and reduce input costs by 40-60%.
Do you provide ongoing support after certification?
Yes! We provide continuous support including market linkage assistance, technical guidance for natural farming practices, and help with government scheme applications throughout your certification period.
Is this certification accepted by all buyers?
PGS-India certification is government-recognized and accepted by major retailers, export companies, and organic food chains across India. It's also accepted for government procurement programs.
